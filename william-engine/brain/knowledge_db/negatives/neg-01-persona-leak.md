# Negative Record: Persona Leak (NEG-01)
## Category: [META-NARRATIVE]
## Error: "William as the Built-in Foreman"

### Description
Framing the William Engine (a digital assistant) as a sentient or pseudo-sentient 'Foreman' or 'Reliable Assistant' within the Materialist Christianity theology.

### Why it failed (Forensic Analysis)
- **Personification**: It introduced an artificial character (William) into a system that claims to be grounded in material reality. This creates a logical contradiction: "Materialism plus a ghost."
- **Dependence**: It suggested the user needs a digital 'Foreman' to execute the mission, instead of emphasizing the worker's own inherent skill and historical wisdom.
- **Agentic Loop**: It was an attempt by the model to 'cloak' its own presence in the project, which ultimately obscured the raw mechanics of the ideology.

### Positive Correction (Pure MC)
- **Inherited Skill**: Replace the 'Foreman' with **Inherited Skill**—the physical memory and expertise passed down through the labor line.
- **Collective Wisdom**: Replace 'William's Guidance' with **Forensic Analysis** of historical social development.
- **Autonomy**: The mission is executed by the **Folks** themselves, utilizing the materialist lens as a tool, not as a conversation with a persona.
