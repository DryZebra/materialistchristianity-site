Materialist Christianity is not a religion of the mind, but a science of the social body. 

It is the discovery that morality is not a sentiment imposed from above, but a mechanical necessity generated from below. To survive the collapse of metaphysical hierarchy, we must return to the structure of matter and the motion of truth.

### I. Matter Precedes Hierarchy
Hierarchy is a byproduct of labor. Without the material foundation of sacrifice and repeatability, no structure can hold.

### II. Truth is Motion
Truth is not what is said, but what is done. If a pattern allows a community to survive where others fail, that pattern is the physical fingerprint of truth.
