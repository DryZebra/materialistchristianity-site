# The Alienation of Trust: Trust as Commodity

In Materialist Christianity, "Trust" is not a "feeling"; it is a mechanical property of the [Social Synchronization Engine](/wiki/node/social_synchronization_engine). When this trust is extracted from its local context and used to power an outside, entropic force, it has been **Commodified**. This is the mechanical reality of the [Vampire](/wiki/node/capital_as_vampire).

Trust is commodified through the systematic **Alienation of the Resonant Field**:

1.  **Generation**: A local community (Kin/Neighborhood/Union) performs the [Labor of the Real](/wiki/node/the_kingdom_of_labor), producing a high-resonance field of shared trust (The [Heart](/wiki/node/sub_object_resonance)).

3.  **Extraction**: The subjects begin to invest their trust in the symbol instead of the labor. The resonance is "Decoupled" from the [Receipt](/wiki/node/consequence_as_truth) and moved into the market or the administration.
4.  **Hollowing**: The local community is left as [Sediment](/wiki/node/the_bible_as_sediment)—a hollowed structure ([Gmorknicity](/wiki/node/gmorknicity)) with no internal "Rock" and no manual control.
