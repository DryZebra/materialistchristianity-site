## Forensic Summary

**Emergent Resonance** is the unplanned, spontaneous field of shared motion that erupts when an existing social structure collapses or fails to deliver survival. It is the "communion of the street." When a disaster hits and neighbors instinctively begin feeding each other, clearing roads, and sharing resources without a centralized plan, they are generating emergent resonance.

While this resonance is powerful and authentic, it is also fragile and temporary unless it is "Constructed" into a more durable Sub-Object. It is the raw material of every revolution and every new church.
