## Forensic Summary

**Constructed Resonance** is a field of human expectation and behavioral pressure that has been built with intent. It is not an accident of nature or crisis, but the result of specific **Moral Labor**. Examples include a carefully designed church liturgy, a legal code, or a union's bylaws.

The purpose of construction is to create a field so strong that it can hold a community's motion even when the individuals within it are tired or doubtful. It is "behavioral engineering" designed for collective survival.
