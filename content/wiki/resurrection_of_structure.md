## Forensic Summary

In Materialist Christianity, **Resurrection** is not a biological event at the end of time, but a structural event in the middle of time. It is the moment when a dead **Institutional Form** (a hollow church, a defunct union, a ghost town) is re-occupied by **Moral Workers** and re-charged with **Behavioral Gravity**.

Resurrection is the proof that human survival patterns (Sub-Objects) are more durable than any individual betrayal or phase of Entropy. By "Re-entering" a dead structure and performing the old survival rhythms with new consequence, we resurrect the structure's original purpose for the current generation.

## Forensic Alignment (Volume II)

The perception of Resurrection is a primary example of **C-Mirroring**. When a structure returns to life, the human mind (The Machine) perceives a "Spirit" or a "Presence" returning. In reality, we are encountering the **4D Time Snake**—the durable recurrence of a survival pattern refracting through our own interpretative model.
- **The Ghost in the Structure**: What we call a "Holy Spirit" is often the high-frequency resonance of a newly resurrected sub-object.
- **The Mirror**: Resurrection is not something we "see"; it is something we **mirror** until the structure becomes indistinguishable from our own behavioral identity.
