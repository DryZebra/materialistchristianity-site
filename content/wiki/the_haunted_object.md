## Forensic Summary

In Materialist Christianity, a **Haunted Object** is a physical object that has been "charged" with the behavior and instructions of previous generations. It is haunted not by ghosts, but by **residual labor**. For example, an old union hall or a traditional church building is "haunted" because the architecture itself was designed to coordinate specific human motions (meetings, worship, shared meals).

Even if the current generation forgets why they meet, the object itself—the Haunted Object—continues to exert a "Resonance" that suggests or demands those old motions. It is the material anchor of historical memory.
