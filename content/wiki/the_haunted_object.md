# The Broken Legacy: The Ghost in the Town

In Materialist Christianity, a **Broken Legacy** is what happens when an old, failed system continues to "haunt" a community long after it has stopped working. We look at the **Abandoned Factories** and the **Empty Storefronts** as the ghosts of a time when the [Vampires](/wiki/node/social_vampirism) were in charge. We don't believe you can build the new world until you deal with the ghosts of the old one.

### I. The Main Problem: Holding on to the Past vs. Building the New
Most people think that if they just "remember the good old days," things will somehow fix themselves. But a working man knows that an old, rusted-out machine in the middle of the shop floor is just taking up space and causing accidents. If you don't clear out the **Haunted Objects**, you can't install the new equipment.

| The Result | The American Fact | What it Means for Us |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rust Belt Reality** | Abandoned mills that still define a town's identity. | The "ghost" is the memory of a lost mission. |
| **Hollow Traditions** | Doing things "the old way" even when they don't work. | The rot continues because we won't let go. |
| **Common Sense Order** | Clearing the site to build a new, stronger home. | We are seeing the results of the historical audit. |

We look at the **Results**. There have been moments in our history, like in the middle of the "Rust Belt," when everyone knew that the old industrial way was dead. But instead of building something new for themselves, folks stayed in the shadow of the **Haunted Object**. This resulted in decades of decay and drug abuse. It’s what keeps the [Working Stiff](/wiki/node/the_materialist_axiom) from seeing the [Kingdom of Labor](/wiki/node/the_kingdom_of_labor).

### II. Where it Comes From: The Ghostly Body
We look at two simple ideas that every American worker understands:

- **The Real Idols**: The Bible calls them "Idols"—things that have eyes but can't see and hands but can't work. For a worker, that means an **Old System that Doesn't Produce**. If you worship the way things *used to be* instead of looking at the [Results](/wiki/node/the_materialist_axiom) of today, you are worshipping a ghost. 
- **The Group Purge**: A lot of leaders want you to stay "Nostalgic" so you won't notice they are siphoning off the current work. We focus on the **Purge**. If the folks in your town are still letting the "Old Names" and the "Old Families" run things just because they always have, then a [Vampire](/wiki/node/social_vampirism) is haunting your future.

### III. The Goal: Clearing the Site for the Future
Our goal is to have every member of our community be a **Ghost-Breaker**. We don't hide what we're after: we want a world where the working people aren't afraid to tear down what is broken to build what is right. We call this the **Unity of the New Build**. A successful focus on The Broken Legacy is measured by one thing: if your community has cleared out the old habits and the old failures to make room for a real, physical [Success](/wiki/node/resurrection_structural), then the plan is verified. The [Church](/wiki/node/the_church_as_structural_anchor) is where we learn the "Exorcism" of common sense.
