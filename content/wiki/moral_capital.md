## Forensic Summary

**Moral Capital** is the materialist equivalent of "Social Capital," but with a focus on **Structural Density**. It is the total amount of shared trust and coordinated motion that a community has built up through generations of **Moral Labor**. It is the "battery" that powers the **Resonance** of a Sub-Object.

Unlike financial capital, which is extracted and moved, moral capital is localized and durable. It is the target of **Social Vampirism**—Capital seeks to drain moral capital from working-class communities (e.g., displacing a tight-knit neighborhood for a highway) because moral capital is the primary resistance to the **Commodity Form**.
