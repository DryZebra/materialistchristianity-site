# The Human-Structure Interface: The Gear and the Body

In Materialist Christianity, the **Human-Structure Interface** is the study of how individual humans interact with the foundational protocols of society—language, law, and survival rituals. We recognize these structures not as arbitrary inventions, but as **Material Discoveries** that have a proven physical track record of stabilizing the species. The interface is the precise point where the biological needs of the human body meet the mechanical requirements of the social gear. Morality, in this context, is the forensic name we give to the **Energy-Efficient Alignment** of the individual with these established structural foundations.

The individual is the operator of the structure, but also its "Patient"—the one who suffers the physical consequences when a social structure fails or is captured by [Social Vampirism](/wiki/node/social_vampirism). Because the primary human need is metabolic stability, we interface with established [Sub-Objects](/wiki/node/sub_objects) to reduce [Social Friction](/wiki/node/the_entropic_dance). We do not "replace" these foundations with newer, unproven ideals; instead, we **Sublate** them. Just as modern physics integrated Newtonian laws into a larger framework, we build upon the biblical foundation, preserving its functional core while expanding its complexity for the [Industrial Real](/wiki/node/the_industrial_real).

### Dynamics of Functional Coordination
- **Function Over Myth (Mark 2:27)**: Christ’s statement that "the sabbath was made for man, and not man for the sabbath" is the ultimate diagnostic for interface priority. It identifies that the structure exists to facilitate human survival, rather than humans existing to serve a hollowed-out [Commodity Form](/wiki/node/the_commodity_form). We are indifferent to the "magic" of the ancient word; we prioritize the fact that its social protocols **Work** to coordinate labor and manage resources.
- **The Zero-Point Baseline (Genesis 1:1)**: The interface begins with the discovery of the substrate itself—the "Beginning" where the fundamental laws of human coordination were first identified and recorded as the [Bible as Sediment](/wiki/node/the_bible_as_sediment). All subsequent history is the recursive application, refinement, or betrayal of this zero-point discovery. To lose the interface with this baseline is to drift into the vacuum of [The Nothing](/wiki/node/the_nothing).
- **Maintenance of the Gear**: The interface requires constant [Moral Labor](/wiki/node/moral_labor). When a structure is distorted by the [Hardness of Heart](/wiki/node/the_hardness_of_heart), the interface becomes high-friction and energy-expensive. Reclaiming the efficiency of the machine requires a **Forensic Audit** to strip away the parasitic layers and restore the individual's ability to participate in the collective metabolic stream (the process of [Resurrection](/wiki/node/resurrection_of_structure)).
