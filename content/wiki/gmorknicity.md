## Forensic Summary

Inspired by the character Gmork from *The Neverending Story*, **Gmorknicity** refers to the active destruction of Sub-Objects and the fields of resonance that bind people together. In the book, Gmork serves "The Nothing" because people who have no shared stories, no shared trust, and no shared structures are easier to manipulate and exploit.

In the materialist framework, Gmorknicity is the effect of **unfettered Capital**. It dissolves the church, the union, and the community, replacing them with individualized "Commodity Forms." It is the force that advocates for the breakdown of tradition and ritual not for "freedom," but for the sake of better extraction.
