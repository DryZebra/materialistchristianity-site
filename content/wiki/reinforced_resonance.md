## Forensic Summary

**Reinforced Resonance** is the accumulation of field density over decades or centuries. It is the reason why a 200-year-old denomination or a long-standing labor union feels "heavier" and more authoritative than a newly formed group. Every time a person complies with the structure, every time a ritual is successfully completed, and every time the structure delivers on a promise (Consequence), the resonance is reinforced.

This density creates a field that can survive extreme periods of "Entropy." It is the protective shell of historical memory that prevents a Sub-Object from dissolving immediately upon a single betrayal.
