## Forensic Summary

In Materialist Christianity, **Liturgy** is the "source code" of the **Church as Sub-Object**. It is not a sequence of magic words, but a **Motion Coordinator**: a script that tells a group of people how to move, when to move, and with whom to move.

A successful liturgy creates a **Survival Rhythm**. It ensures that even when people are confused or afraid, the community's movement (standing, kneeling, eating, sharing) remains intact. Liturgy is the technology for embedding structural memory into the bodies of the congregants, making the group resilient to the "Entropy" of isolation.
