# Liturgy as Motion Coordinator: The Protocol of Sync

In Materialist Christianity, **Liturgy** is not a set of mystical incantations but a **Motion Coordinator**—a material synchronization protocol designed to align the movements of the human collective. We recognize liturgy as the "Software" that runs on the [Church as Structural Anchor](/wiki/node/the_church_as_structural_anchor). Its primary function is to embed the [Newtonian Baseline](/wiki/node/the_bible_as_sediment) into the very bodies of the producers, moving them out of the entropic noise of individualized interest and into the coordinated field of the [Kingdom of Labor](/wiki/node/the_kingdom_of_labor).

We treat liturgy as **Collective Synchronization**. Just as a group of workers must move in perfect sync to lift a heavy beam, a community must move in sync (Ritual, Meal, Rest, Song) to resist the entropy of [Social Friction](/wiki/node/the_entropic_dance). Liturgy is the technology for making the group's behavior predictable and reliable. By repeating the same motions—the Eucharist, the Sabbath, the Creed—the collective preserves the functional discoveries of the [Beginning](/bible/gen/1), ensuring they remain accessible even during systemic collapses or [Liberal Drift](/wiki/node/the_human_structure_interface).

- **The Protocol of the Meal (Luke 22:19)**: Christ's institution of the shared meal was the ultimate act of **Liturgy as Motion Coordinator**. His command—"This do in remembrance of me"—was a directive of **Physical Repetition**. He identified that the [Shared Trust](/wiki/node/shared_trust) of the collective is maintained through the repeated motion of the "Meal," the ultimate coordinator of human metabolic survival.
- **Structural Momentum**: Liturgy creates a social flywheel. Once the motions are established, they possess [Subject-Independent Resonance](/wiki/node/subject_independent_resonance), allowing the structure to persist even when individual commitment wafts. This is the mechanism by which the species "records" its successful survival protocols into the substrate of physical behavior.
