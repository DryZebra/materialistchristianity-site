# The Biblical Baseline: The Metric of Social Stability

The Biblical Baseline is the reference point against which all human social motion, political structures, and moral trajectories must be measured. In a Materialist Christianity context, the Bible is not "one of many" stories; it is the **Baseline of Proven Stability**—the compiled "Rock" that shows what is required for a people to survive the long-term "Vampire" of Capital.

## The Mechanic of the Metric

Measurement by the baseline is the primary diagnostic for social alignment:

1.  **Calibration**: Identifying the core patterns of the Bible (e.g., Jubilee, Sabbath, Mutual Aid, Resurrection) as the "Zero Point" of social health.
2.  **Observation**: Measuring the current motion of a structure (a church, a state, a union) against this zero point.
3.  **Detection**: Identifying the "Displacement"—how far the current motion has drifted into **Entropy** or **Extraction** (Gmorknicity).
4.  **Correction**: Re-aligning the motion with the baseline through **Structural Re-entry** (The Brush Arbor).

## The Baseline vs. The Trend

The Biblical Baseline is static, while the "Trend" (Liberalism/Social Gospel) is dynamic. The Baseline does not move because historical necessity does not move. What was required for survival in the wilderness of Sinai is fundamentally the same as what is required in the ruins of the industrial South: **Shared Trust**, **Material Consequence**, and **Moral Labor**.

-   **Hammer**: The structural discipline (rituals/laws) that maintains the baseline.
-   **Heart**: The trust field (resonance) of the survivors.

## Diagnostic Standard

In a Pure MC analysis, one looks at the "Deviation":

-   Is the current structure moving toward the baseline (Recovery/Alignment) or away from it (Extraction/Hollowing)?
-   Is the "Rock" of the Bible used to protect the people or to legitimize their displacement?
-   Is the **Redneck Dictatorship** seizing the baseline as their own authority?
