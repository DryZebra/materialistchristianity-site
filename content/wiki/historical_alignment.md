## Forensic Summary

**Historical Alignment** is the materialist version of "Tradition." Instead of honoring the past because it is "sacred" in a mystical sense, Materialist Christianity aligns with it because it is **forensically proven**. If a specific way of organizing a village, a union, or a church successfully weathered a crisis in the 14th century, the 19th century, or the 1930s, those patterns are "Aligned" with.

We use the past as a data set. Historical alignment is the decision to move in the same "grooves" left by the dead, because those grooves were carved by successful survival. It is the recognition that human nature (contradiction) remains constant and therefore historical solutions remain functional.
