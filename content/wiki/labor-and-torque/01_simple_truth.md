# The Simple Truth: A Technical Audit of the Real

Materialist Christianity is not a "religion" of feelings, theories, or distant hopes. It is the **Forensic Recognition of the Real**. It is the discovery that how we live, how we treat our neighbor, and how we coordinate our labor is not a matter of "opinion"—it is a set of **Rules that Work** or **Mistakes that Cost Us**.

## I. The Foundation First

You do not build a structure from the roof down. You start with the **Physical Foundation** and the **Hard Work**. In this project, we recognize that the **Productive Class**—those who grow the food, build the tools, and maintain the towns—are the primary eyewittneses to the **Truth**. Talk is cheap; the **Result** is the only valid metric. Any social plan that ignores the physical reality of the 15-man shop or the local neighborhood is destined for **Decay**.

## II. The Pressure Test: Survival as Verification

Truth is not what a politician, a banker, or an "expert" tells you. Truth is what remains when the world is in crisis and the pressure is on. If a specific behavior results in a stable family, a protected neighbor, and a **Strong Community**, then that is the **Physical Fact** of truth. We do not argue with the world; we simply measure the **Receipts** of what actually keeps us alive.
