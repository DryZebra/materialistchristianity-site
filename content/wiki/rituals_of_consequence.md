## Forensic Summary

A **Ritual of Consequence** is a repeatable action that results in a material improvement in survival or structural density. In the Materialist framework, if a ritual (like a prayer meeting) does not lead to a physical consequence (like feeding the hungry, organizing a strike, or repairing a roof), it is not a ritual—it is a **Ritual of Continuity** (a mask for death).

Authentic rituals are the mechanisms through which **Moral Labor** is coordinated. They are "liturgies of survival" where the truth of the action is measured by the fruit it bears in the industrial real.
