## Forensic Summary

**Subject-Independent Resonance** is the hallmark of a high-torque social structure. It is a field of behavioral pressure that persists even if no single individual within the group "believes" in it. This happens when the resonance is so deeply anchored in **Sub-Objects** (mass collective behavior, physical infrastructure, historical precedent) that it becomes a feature of the "Industrial Real."

You walk into a court of law and lower your voice; you do this not because you admire the judge (Subjective), but because the structural resonance of the court (Subject-Independent) demands it through the weight of its established motion.
