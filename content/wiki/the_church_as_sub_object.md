## Forensic Summary

In this framework, the **Church** is defined as the ultimate **Sub-Object**. It is a relational infrastructure composed of people, buildings, shared trust, and survival rhythms. While it has a metaphysical mask, its material function is to coordinate human behavior across long spans of time and across geographical distance.

A church is functional only when it acts as a "Behavioral Gravity" well, forcing its members into motions of mutual aid, moral labor, and shared resistance. When a church stops these motions, it becomes an empty "Institutional Form"—a shell of a Sub-Object that no longer carries the mass of the people.
