# Non-Extractive Pooling: Material Mutual Aid

In the Materialist Christianity framework, **Capital is a Vampire**. It exists by extracting surplus labor from the neighborhood and moving it to a central "coffin" (The Bank/The State) where the neighbors can no longer touch it. 

**Non-Extractive Pooling** is the mechanical defense against this extraction. It is how a neighborhood builds **Trust Capital** that the extractor cannot touch.

## The Mechanism: The Shared Tool Shed

1.  **Stop the Leak**: Every time a neighbor buys a tool they only use once a year, they are leaking capital to the extractor.
2.  **Pool the Resource**: Instead of 10 neighbors buying 10 lawnmowers, they buy one high-quality, professional-grade machine and store it in a **Shared Tool Shed**.
3.  **Physical Solidarity**: The shared shed requires a **Maintenance Contract**. Neighbors must agree on who cleans the tool, who repairs it, and who stores it. This is **Moral Labor** in its purest form.
4.  **The Result**: The "saved" money stays in the local circuit. It can be used for emergencies, medical bills, or community infrastructure. The extractor is "starved" of 9 lawnmower sales, and the neighbors are enriched by shared trust.

## Literal Mechanism of Wealth Retention

Pooling is not about "charity"; it is about **Internalizing the Economy**. 
-   **Old Way**: Work for the Boss -> Get Paid -> Pay the Extractor for Tools/Food -> Stay Broke.
-   **New Way**: Work for the Boss -> Get Paid -> Pool Resources with Neighbors -> Retention of Surplus -> Local Resilience.

By pooling resources, you create a **Buffer Zone** where the "General" rule of the neighborhood provides for the "Particular" need of the individual. This is the material baseline of the **Kingdom of Labor**.
