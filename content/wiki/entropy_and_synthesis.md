## Forensic Summary

**Entropy** is the natural tendency of a Sub-Object to lose its behavioral gravity over time, especially when it is no longer being actively reinforced by labor or consequence. In this state, rituals become hollow, laws become jokes, and the structure of a society begins to "drift."

**Synthesis** is the re-entry of labor into a decayed structure to revitalize it or the creation of a new structure from the wreckage of the old. It is the active counter-force to structural drift. In Materialist Christianity, synthesis is the primary duty of the worker—taking historical patterns that have drifted into entropy and re-charging them with new motion and consequence.
