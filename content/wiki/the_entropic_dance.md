The Entropic Dance was a theoretical framework that proposed that morality is simply the minimization of entropy in a closed system.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Forensic Testimony**: [The Entropic Dance: The Failure of the Engineered](/essays/the_entropic_dance)

### Core Claims:
- **Thermodynamic Morality**: The original claim was that "Good" is what resists the heat death of social systems.
- **Structural Failure**: The framework failed because it treated humans as logical agents rather than biological "Machines" with inherited and adaptive wiring.
- **Physics in a Vacuum**: It ignored the **Industrial Real**—the material conditions, labor, and history that warp the field of possibility.
- **Synthesis Point**: Materialist Christianity was born from the "post-mortem" of the Entropic Dance. It kept the physics-first approach but added the **LHC Model** and the Christian archetype as the grounding wire.

### Dialectical Integration
- **LHC Model**: In the **LHC Model**, the Entropic Dance attempted a direct H-to-L mapping without the "C-Reflector" (Co-Construct). This resulted in an unstable field that collapsed under the load of human trauma.
- **Residue**: The remnants of the Entropic Dance survive as the "physics-base" of Materialist Christianity.
