The Well Water Principle describes how power, legitimacy, morality, and consciousness continue to flow through a system even after visible institutions lose trust or credibility.

### Core Claims:
- **You are not independent from a system you are still materially downstream from.**
- Rejection of authority does not stop power flow; distrust of institutions does not sever dependency.
- Independence requires rupture at the level of material dependency.
- If you rely on the outputs of a system, you are structurally downstream from it.
- **The Water Chain**: Rain → Soil → Aquifer → Well → Mouth. Power flows from its source to your consciousness through material pipes.

### Axiomatic Root (Volume I)
The Well Water Principle is the forensic application of **Sub-Objects**. Every "pipe" in the chain is a sub-object—a relational structure that dictates the movement of the real (water/energy/labor).
- **Material Dependency**: We do not just "believe" in the sub-object; we rely on it. This reliance generates the **Behavioral Gravity** that makes the system move.
- **Rupture**: Independence from a system requires a literal rupture of the material sub-objects that feed you.
