## Forensic Summary

**Social Vampirism** is the large-scale application of the **Commodity as Vampire** mechanic. It is the process by which Capital target's a community's **Moral Capital** (its shared trust, local institutions, and survival rhythms) for extraction. This is seen in gentrification, the privatization of public space, and the destruction of local churches or union halls.

Capital cannot build these things; it can only "bite" them, draining the structural density that once allowed the working class to coordinate their own survival. The result of social vampirism is a field of **The Nothing**, where once there was a thriving Sub-Object.
