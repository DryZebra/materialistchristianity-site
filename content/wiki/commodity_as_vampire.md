## Forensic Summary

This node defines the **Commodity Form** as a parasite. In Materialist Christianity, a Sub-Object (like a church or a holiday) is "alive" when it coordinates human survival. When Capital captures that object, it is "bitten": the real behavioral life is drained out to create financial value, leaving behind a hollow institutional corpse.

The "Vampire" analogy is forensic: Capital cannot create its own resonance; it can only feed on the resonance built by **Moral Labor**. It drains the "Moral Capital" of a community, leaving them individualized and susceptible to **The Nothing**.
