## Forensic Summary

In Materialist Christianity, **Apostolic Succession** is not a mystical "spark" passed through fingertips. It is **Apostolic Motion**: the physical continuity of a specific set of survival behaviors from one body to the next. It is the unbroken chain of **Moral Labor** and shared trust that connects the modern worker to the historical collective.

Succession is valid only when the **Motion** is valid. If a bishop claims succession but does not move in alignment with the historical "Sediment" of sacrifice and struggle (Rejection of the Commodity Form), then the succession is broken—regardless of the institutional paperwork.
