## Forensic Summary

**Sacramental Materialism** is the recognition that the "Sacraments" of the Church (Baptism, Communion, etc.) are actually material technologies for social engineering. In Materialist Christianity, Communion is not a mystical ritual about a ghost, but a **Ritual of Consequence** where the group shares food, synchronizes their motion, and reaffirms their commitment to mutual aid.

A sacrament is "valid" only if it produces a material effect in the **Industrial Real**. If you take communion but do not provide for your neighbor, the sacrament is forensically void. The material is the measures of the spiritual.
