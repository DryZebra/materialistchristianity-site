## Forensic Summary

**Motion in Isolation** is the primary litmus test for structural integrity in Materialist Christianity. It asks: *What does a person do when there is no social reward, no fear of public punishment, and no one watching?*

If a behavior (like a ritual, an ethical choice, or a discipline) persisted only because of social pressure or branding, it will vanish in isolation. If it persists in isolation, it is because it has been integrated into the individual's "Behavioral Gravity" or because it is a necessary survival habit. This "Isolated Motion" is the only portion of a belief system that can be considered real and durable. It is the residue of labor that has become reflex.
