## Forensic Summary

**Antagonistic Resonance** is the field effect of resistance. It occurs when a group organizes its motion in direct opposition to an existing Sub-Object or field of power. The classic example is a **Strike**: a coordinated refusal to move according to the Capitalist schedule, instead creating a new, competitive rhythm of the picket line.

Antagonistic resonance is necessary for breaking the "Behavioral Gravity" of a captured structure. To fight a dominant field, you must not only dissent in thought (Subject) but create a physical field of competitive pressure (Sub-Object).
