# Antagonistic Resonance: The Momentum of the Strike

In Materialist Christianity, **Antagonistic Resonance** is a forensic term for collective structural momentum. It describes the state where multiple human nodes coordinate their [Antagonistic Labor](/wiki/node/antagonistic_labor) toward a single goal—the dismantling of a parasitic, extractive structure. We treat this as a **Social Force** generated by a strike, a boycott, or a collective refusal to participate in the [Commodity Form](/wiki/node/the_commodity_form). To break the [Behavioral Gravity](/wiki/node/behavioral_gravity) of a captured system, a community must align its antagonistic motion to physically overcome the structural resistance of the status quo.

Historical resistance within the biblical sediment functions as a resonance engine. By providing a shared survival protocol of absolute liability and mutual trust, it allows the masses to resonate with a force capable of crushing extractive empires. This is not a "vibe" or a subjective feeling, but a material coordination equivalent to a group of people pushing a heavy load in unison. Antagonistic Resonance is the counter-force to [Social Friction](/wiki/node/the_entropic_dance), used to isolate the [Hardness of Heart](/wiki/node/the_hardness_of_heart) and reclaim the resources of the metabolic baseline.

### Mechanics of Coordinated Force
- **The Shout of the People (Joshua 6:20)**: The account of the fall of Jericho is a forensic documentation of coordinated resonance. When the people shouted with a "Great Shout," the wall fell down flat. This describes a collective body whose internal coordination was high enough to physically overwhelm the structural integrity (The Wall) of a closed, extractive system. The "Shout" is the material signal that aligns the metabolic output of thousands into a singular, destructive beam of social energy.
- **Structural Multiplier (Matthew 18:20)**: The promise that "where two or three are gathered" there is a manifestations of power is the forensic definition of **Resonant Multiplier**. The "Name" (the shared protocol) is what allows the latent power of the functional baseline to manifest as a material force in the [Industrial Real](/wiki/node/the_industrial_real). Coordination is the only tool capable of overcoming the energy-expensive friction of [The Nothing](/wiki/node/the_nothing).
- **Sublation by Force**: We resonate to **Sublate**. We use the force of our collective antagonism to dismantle the layers of [Social Vampirism](/wiki/node/social_vampirism) so that the functional substrate of the [Kingdom of Labor](/wiki/node/the_kingdom_of_labor) can be restored. Any resonance that does not lead to the building of new load-bearing structures is forensically identified as "Noise" and will eventually dissipate into metabolic exhaustion.
