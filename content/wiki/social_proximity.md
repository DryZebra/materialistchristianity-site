# Social Proximity: The Simulation of Presence

Social Proximity is the term for the [Commodity](/wiki/node/the_commodity_form) version of **Presence**. It is the sensation of being seen, mentioned, or included in a social structure, without the material [behavioral gravity](/wiki/node/behavioral_gravity) of a literal neighborhood.

## The Mechanic of the Simulation

As the [Social Vampire](/wiki/node/social_vampirism) drains the structural mass of a community, it creates a void of **Presence**. It then sells the simulation of that presence back to the isolated subject:

1.  **Hollow out the Local**: The destruction of the [Brush Arbor](/wiki/node/the_brush_arbor) and the neighborhood, leaving the subject isolated.
2.  **Market the Vibe**: The symbols of belonging (The Heart) are turned into digital content—pure resonance detached from physical neighborliness.
3.  **Sell Proximity**: The subject pays for the sensation of being "close" to the source of resonance (e.g., a streamer, a brand, an influencer). 
4.  **The Transaction**: The subject exchanges their [Labor](/wiki/node/the_kingdom_of_labor) (cash) for a temporary hit of [Social Capital](/wiki/node/moral_capital) that expires as soon as the feed stops.

## Presence vs. Proximity: The Structural Support (NEC 344.30)

In a Pure MC diagnostic, we apply the **3-Foot Support Rule** (derived from the NEC) to social structures:

-   **Presence (Supported)**: A neighbor within physical distance (3 feet) who carries your load. Like a code-compliant conduit, the connection is **Secured and Supported** by the physical reality of mutual risk.
-   **Proximity (Unsupported)**: A simulation (The Feed) that makes you *feel* connected over a distance, but lacks any structural support at the terminal point. It is a vibrating, loose conduit that will fail under any material load.

## Diagnostic Standard

Social Proximity is the diagnostic used to identify an **Unsupported Structure**:

-   **Is it Supported?**: Does the relationship have the physical support of presence within "3 feet" (Neighborhood), or is it an unsupported run of pure attention?
-   **Box or Loop?**: Is the connection reaching a Junction Box (a place of real termination and survival), or is it just looping back into the [Feed](/wiki/node/social_proximity)?
-   **The Voltage**: When the crisis hits, can the structure carry the voltage of survival? Proximity shorts out immediately; Presence is grounded.

> [!CAUTION]
> Social Proximity is a form of [Borrowed Happiness](/wiki/node/borrowed_happiness). It is used to mask the pain of structural isolation. To defeat it, one must reclaim **Presence** by rebuilding the [Brush Arbor](/wiki/node/the_brush_arbor)—the only structure that can provide off-grid support.
