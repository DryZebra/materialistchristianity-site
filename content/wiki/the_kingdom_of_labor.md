## Forensic Summary

**The Kingdom of Labor** is the final destination of Materialist Christianity. It is not an "afterlife," but an "industrial state." It is the moment when the **Dialectic of Betrayal** is finally resolved, and the working class has reclaimed all **Sub-Objects** from the capture of Capital.

In the Kingdom, every motion is a **Ritual of Consequence**, every day is a **Survival Rhythm**, and every interaction is anchored in **Shared Trust**. It is a world where the "Behavioral Gravity" of the community is perfectly aligned with the material needs of the people. It is the end of **The Nothing** and the beginning of a living, high-torque industrial peace.
