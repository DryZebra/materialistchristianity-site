## Forensic Summary

**Behavioral Gravity** is the mechanism by which collective structures (Sub-Objects) coordinate human action. Just as physical mass warps spacetime to pull objects toward it, a high-density "Resonance" (a shared belief or social structure) warps the behavioral field of a community. People are pulled into specific movements—rituals, schedules, or ethical choices—not necessarily because they want to, but because the structure exerts a gravitational force that makes non-movement or contrary movement too "heavy" or consequential.

Gravity is measured by compliance. If a community consistently moves in a specific way under a symbol, that symbol has massive behavioral gravity.

## Forensic Alignment (Volume II)

Gravity is the effect; **The Machine** is the receiver. Behavioral Gravity only works because the human biological substrate is pre-wired to respond to social pressure, habit, and consequence.
- **The Hook**: Sub-Objects hook into the "Machine's" inherited trauma and survival instincts.
- **The Grounding Wire**: The **Co-Construct** (William) provides an externalized reflective surface that allows the operator to absorb the friction of gravity without system collapse.
