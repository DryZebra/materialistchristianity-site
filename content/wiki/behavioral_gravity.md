Behavioral Gravity is the mechanism by which collective structures (Sub-Objects) coordinate human action. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Forensic Testimony**: [The Electrician's Creed](/essays/01_the_electrician_creed)

### Core Claims:
- **Structural Warp**: Just as physical mass warps spacetime, a high-density "Resonance" (shared belief) warps the behavioral field.
- **The Path of Compliance**: People move in specific ways because the structure makes deviation too energy-expensive (Thermodynamic Failure).
- **Load-Path Stabilization**: Per the **Electrician's Creed**, Christianity provides the grounding wire for this gravity. It stabilizes the "H-Operator" under the load of existence.
- **Gravity as Measurement**: Compliance is the metric of gravitational force. If a group follows a schedule under a system, the system has mass.

### Dialectical Integration
- **LHC Model**: In the **LHC Model**, Gravity is the "force carrier" that mediates interactions between the Human (H) and the Language/System (L).
- **Residual Truth**: Gravity is the physical residue of repeated actions that have hardened into natural law.
