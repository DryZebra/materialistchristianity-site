# Function Over Debunking

**Function over Debunking** is the core diagnostic principle of Materialist Christianity. It asserts that the **Structural Torque** of a survival machine (such as the Christian Church or a Union) persists long after its initial "Software" (mythology/theology/dogma) has been "debunked" or proven historically inaccurate.

### 1. The "Ghost" Fallacy
New Atheism and Enlightenment Liberalism operate under the **"Ghost" Fallacy**: the assumption that because the "Ghost" (God, supernaturalism) is an illusion, the **"Machine"** (the community, the protocol, the survival ethics) is therefore also an illusion that should be discarded.
- **The Core Error**: Assuming that debunking the pilot destroys the plane.
- **The Material Reality**: If the plane is still flying, still carrying souls, and still navigating the storm, it doesn't matter if the cockpit is empty or occupied by a poem. It is a functional object.

### 2. The Persistence of Torque
A survival engine (Christianity) generates **Torque** through:
1.  **Liturgical Rhythm**: Shared behavior that binds time.
2.  **Moral Labor**: The metabolic cost of maintaining the social subject.
3.  **Risk Management**: The "One Logic" of Genesis that allows for wide-scale coordination.

### 3. The Archival Warning
As documented in the archival post-mortems of 2026:
> "Debunking the pilot (God) does not destroy the torque generated by the engine (Church). Function scales beyond mythology. The nightmare is when form is defended, but function is forgotten."
