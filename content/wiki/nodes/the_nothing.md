# The Nothing

**The Nothing** is a conceptual identifier for **[the_vacuum](/wiki/wiki/nodes/the_vacuum)**. In the Materialist Christianity lens, it represents the state of absolute non-motion or the terminal point of alienation where the subject ceases to be a functional participant in the social metabolic process.

It is the void left when morality is extracted and the structural anchor is removed.
