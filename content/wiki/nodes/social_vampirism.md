# Social Vampirism

**Social Vampirism** is the diagnostic mechanism for **[[capital_as_vampire]]**. It describes the extraction of "Life" (labor time, attention, and cognitive energy) from one subject to fuel the growth of an inorganic structure (Capital, Algorithms, or Extractive Institutions).
