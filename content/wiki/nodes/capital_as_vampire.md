# Capital (The Vampire Diagnostic)

## I. DIAGNOSTIC SUMMARY
In the Materialist Christianity lens, **Capital** is not merely money; it is a metabolic parasite. It is "Dead Labor" that lives only by sucking "Living Labor" ([Moral Labor](/wiki/nodes/moral_labor)). It is the force of **Gmorknicity** applied to the species' survival resonance.

1.  **Extraction over Maintenance**: Capital seeks the "Shortcut." It extracts the value of a [The Sub Object](/wiki/nodes/the_sub_object) (e.g., the safety of a neighborhood) and transforms it into an exchangeable commodity (Rent), without contributing to the maintenance of the field.
2.  **Siphoning Resonance**: It captures symbols (The Cross, The Flag) to generate a false [Sub Object Resonance](/wiki/nodes/sub_object_resonance). This siphons the subject's trust into a system that provides zero material return.
3.  **The Dead Hand**: Capital is the "Fossilization" of motion. It turns liquid [Motion As Reality](/wiki/nodes/motion_as_reality) into solid, unchangeable "Assets."

## III. SURVIVAL "WHY"
For the social species, Capital is the "Entropy" of the group. If too much of the group's labor is fossilized into Capital, the group loses its ability to react to environmental shifts. It becomes rigid, brittle, and enters [Rupture](/wiki/nodes/rupture).
