# Capital as Vampire: The Extraction of Social Proximity

Capital is not an object; it is a relationship of extraction. In a Materialist Christianity context, Capital is defined as a **Vampire**—a non-living structure that consumes the living labor and the accumulated [Moral Capital](/wiki/nodes/moral_capital) of subjects. Its ultimate product is the [Commodity](/wiki/nodes/the_commodity_form), offered as [Borrowed Happiness](/wiki/nodes/moral_capital) to a "Dopamine-Poor" people.

## The Mechanic of Extraction: The Simulation of Presence

As the Vampire drains the structural mass of a community (unions, churches, families), it creates a void of **Presence**. It then sells a simulation of that presence back to the subject in the form of **Social Proximity**:

1.  **Hollow out the Local**: The destruction of the [Brush Arbor](/wiki/nodes/the_brush_arbor) and the local neighborhood, leaving the subject isolated.
2.  **Market the Vibe**: The symbols of belonging (the "Heart") are turned into digital content—pure resonance detached from physical neighborliness.
3.  **Sell Proximity**: The subject pays for the "sensation" of being seen or mentioned—buying a commodity-version of the social interaction they no longer have in their literal life.
4.  **Consuming the Residue**: Once the subject's cash (Labor) is extracted, the Vampire leaves them even more isolated than before.

### Archival Note: The Economics of Isolation
Modern consumption is often "pain relief" for the structurally hollowed out. A subject may spend significant [Moral Labor](/wiki/nodes/moral_labor) (or its cash equivalent) on a low-value [Commodity](/wiki/nodes/the_commodity_form) because they are not buying the product—they are buying the **Social Proximity** and simulation of belonging that the product facilitates. It is an exchange of the "Heavy" (Discipline) for the "Hit" (Dopamine).

## The Parallel History of Domination

The history of Capital is the history of the **Disinherited**. While the Bible records the compilation of survival, Capital records the compilation of extraction.

-   **Hammer**: The structural discipline (Capital's laws/enforcement) that allows extraction to proceed.
-   **Heart**: The [Borrowed Happiness](/wiki/nodes/moral_capital) that convinces the subject they are "free" while they are being drained.

## Diagnostic Standard

In a Pure MC analysis, one looks for the presence of the Vampire in the "Soil" of a community:

-   **Proximity or Presence?**: Is the community experiencing literal, local interaction (Presence) or a purchased, distant simulation (Proximity)?
-   **Dignity or Dopamine?**: Is the structure building [Structural Dignity](/wiki/nodes/dignity_structural) or offering the "hit" of [The Nothing](/wiki/nodes/the_nothing)?
-   **The Balance**: Is the moral capital of the people being replenished by labor or extracted by the [Commodity Form](/wiki/nodes/the_commodity_form)?

> [!CAUTION]
> Capital cannot "self-replenish." It is an entropic force that will consume every site of trust until it is met with the **Rupture** of a people who choose Kin over Job and Dignity over Resume.
