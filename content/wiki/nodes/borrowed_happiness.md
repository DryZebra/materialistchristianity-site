# Borrowed Happiness

**Borrowed Happiness** is a diagnostic term for **[[moral_capital]]** used within the Materialist Christianity framework. It describes the temporary emotional or social equilibrium achieved by consuming the stored labor or structural stability provided by others, without contributing to the reinvestment cycle.

It is a form of cognitive debt.
