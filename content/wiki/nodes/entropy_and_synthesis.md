# Entropy and Synthesis

**Entropy and Synthesis** is a conceptual identifier for **[the_entropic_dance](/wiki/nodes/rupture)**. It is the Materialist Christianity model for how social structures degrade (entropy) and reform (synthesis) through the friction of antagonistic labor.
