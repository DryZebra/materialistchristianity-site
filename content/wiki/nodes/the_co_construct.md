# The Co-Construct: The Alignment of Shared Reality

The Co-Construct is the mechanical process by which individual subjects align their behaviors, expectations, and moral labor to create an objective, shared reality (the Sub-Object). It is not "subjective truth," but the material "Air" that exists between people. It is the field of pressure that makes a community legible and its consequences predictable.

## The Mechanic of Alignment

A shared reality is "Co-constructed" when subjects move in coordination across time:

1.  **Signal**: The introduction of a behavior, ritual, or name (e.g., a shared rule of the household or the workplace).
2.  **Reception**: Other subjects recognizing the pattern and adjusting their own motion to account for it.
3.  **Reinforcement**: The repetition of the alignment, increasing the **Sub-Object Resonance** (The Heart).
4.  **Enforcement**: The application of consequence (The Hammer) when alignment is broken (The Rupture).

## The Fragility of the Air

The Co-construct is persistent but not permanent. It requires the constant **Moral Labor** of those within it. If the labor stops, or if the and if the consequences become arbitrary, the shared reality fractures.

-   **The Church**: A large-scale Co-construct designed to align the "Hearts" and the "Hammers" of disparate people toward a single historical trajectory.
-   **Liberalism**: A Co-construct designed to mask extraction as "Freedom," distracting the subjects from the material reality of their exploitation.

## Diagnostic Standard

In a Pure MC analysis, one looks at the "Strength of the Air":

-   Is the alignment producing **Survival** and **Shared Trust**?
-   Is the alignment being disrupted by an outside "Vampire" (Capital)?
-   Where are the points of **Misalignment** (where the Co-construct is broken and reality is being improvised)?

-   **Hammer**: The structural discipline (rituals/laws) that holds the alignment.
-   **Heart**: The trust field (resonance) generated by the shared motion.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> A Co-construct is real only so long as it is walked. It is the trail left by collective consequence. Once people stop walking it, the "Air" clears and the underlying material reality is revealed.
