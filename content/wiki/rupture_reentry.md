## Forensic Summary

**Rupture** occurs when a Sub-Object is betrayed (converted into a Commodity Form or hijacked by corrupt interests) and the "Shared Trust" of the group collapses. **Re-entry** is the active decision of a "Moral Worker" to walk back into that broken structure—not to "save" its institutional mask, but to re-activate the **Survival Rhythms** that once made it holy.

Re-entry does not require the institution's permission. It is a materialist act of reclaiming the field of resonance. One does not wait for a church or a union to be "pure" before entering; one enters to provide the labor that *generates* purity through consequence.
