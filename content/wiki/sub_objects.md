Sub-Objects are the foundational building blocks of social reality—relational structures that exist only when behavior is coordinated across multiple people over time.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Forensic Testimony**: [The Materialist Axiom](/wiki/node/the_materialist_axiom)

### Core Claims:
- **Autonomy of Structure**: Sub-objects (currency, law, schedules) are not "tools"; they are autonomous machines that command the hands that built them.
- **Relational Mass**: A sub-object gains mass (Behavioral Gravity) through repetition. If you disregard the "Monday-Friday" week, the system still punishes your motion.
- **LHC Model Integration**: In the **LHC Model**, the Sub-Object is the "L-Reflector" (Language/Ledger). It is the code that allows "H-Operators" (Humans) to coordinate behavior into a "Co-Constructed" field.
- **Material Dependency**: Per the **Well Water Principle**, you are materially downstream from the sub-objects that provide your survival (The Aquifer).

### Dialectical Integration
- **Shadow vs Body**: The Sub-Object is the shadow (Abstraction) of real labor. In a terminal system (Capital), the shadow eventually begins to consume the body (Productive Base).
- **Resurrection of Structure**: To reclaim a sub-object is to reanimate "dead labor" into living collective service.
