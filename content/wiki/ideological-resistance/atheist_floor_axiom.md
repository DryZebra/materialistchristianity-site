# The Analytical Floor

The **Analytical Floor** is the starting point for Materialist Christianity's forensic framework. To analyze the historical success of biblical structures objectively, we must start at the absolute ground level: what is physically and technically verifiable.

## The Floor of the Real

We start our analysis by looking exclusively at physical output.

1.  **The Rejection of Idealism**: We do not evaluate the Church based on "Visions" or theoretical "Metaphors." We evaluate it based on **Historical Truth** and **Physical Work**. Did the community feed the poor? Did they successfully organize against Roman extraction?
2.  **The Honesty of the Mechanic**: Our working assumption is that the biblical text contains the blueprints for fixing the broken world. To understand those blueprints, we must read them as historical manuals for human survival, focusing on the literal tasks performed rather than spiritual analogies.
3.  **The Structural Requirement**: You cannot perform a forensic audit of the modern globalist system if you do not have a grounded, historical baseline to compare it against. You must start at the **Materialist Floor** to see how ancient communities effectively pushed back against their own globalist empires.

## Summary

The Analytical Floor is the boundary line. It ensures our analysis never drifts away from the physical reality of the working man into the clouds of abstract philosophy.
