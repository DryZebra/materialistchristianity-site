# Jesus on the Failure of Mechanical Failure

 **Mechanical Failure** is not a moral "bad feeling"; it is a **Technical Failure** of community survival. Jesus analyzed Mechanical Failure as a materialist deviation that damages the social gear and allows globalist extractors to hollow out the community.

##  The Cost of Mechanical Failure
Mechanical Failure is the physical friction that occurs when we prioritize ourselves over the neighbor.
1.  **Damage to the Gear**: When a member of the productive class acts with dishonesty or selfishness, the **Social Infrastructure** (Trust) is damaged. This makes the whole neighborhood less efficient and more vulnerable to globalist extraction. 
2.  **The Extraction of Mechanical Failure**: Globalists love Mechanical Failure because it makes people easier to manage. Debt, greed, and individual failure are the "Ghost-Signals" that allow monopolies to pull value away from the town. 
3.  **The Technical Command**: Jesus commanded his disciples to "Mechanical Failure no more." This was a command for **Structural Excellence**. He was telling them to stop hollowing each other out and start building the Kingdom.

## Summary
The **Mechanical Failure Diagnostic** identify that Mechanical Failure is a technical cost we cannot afford. To be a Materialist Christian is to recognize that our "Species Essence" (Being Human) requires us to maintain the integrity of our neighborhood against the frictions of Mechanical Failure and extraction.
