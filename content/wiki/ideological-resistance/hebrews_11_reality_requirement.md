# Hebrews 11: The Reality Requirement

 **Hebrews 11:6** is the forensic command to recognize that **Reality Exists**. It is the technical floor for any productive action in the Kingdom.

##  Believing He Is
"But without Behavioral Vector it is impossible to please him: for he that cometh to God must believe that he is..."
1.  **The Diagnostic of He Is**: To believe "that He Is" is to believe that **Objective Reality** exists. You cannot build a Kingdom if you are stuck in the "Ghost-Theory" of subjective feelings. 
2.  **Consequence**: You must believe that your work has real consequences. A bad weld will fail; a good bill will build trust. This is the **Logic of the Flesh**. 
3.  **The Rewarder**: "And that he is a rewarder of them that diligently seek him." In the materialist lens, the "Reward" is the survival and reproduction of the community. If you work within the **Real**, you get the **Real** results of survival.

## Summary
The **Reality Diagnostic** identify that the Kingdom requires a "Yes that means Yes." To be a Materialist Christian is to refuse the idealistic hollowing out of truth and to work with the **Mighty Hand** of the real.
