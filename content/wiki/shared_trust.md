## Forensic Summary

**Shared Trust** is treated as a material resource in this framework. It is not a "feeling" or a "vibe"; it is the structural confidence that a community has in its shared Sub-Objects. Trust is built through the repeated delivery of consequence (e.g., "The deacon said he would fix the roof, and the roof was fixed").

In the Materialist framework, trust is the fuel that allows **Resonance** to thicken. When trust is high, the "Behavioral Gravity" of a community is strong enough to resist external shocks. When trust is low (usually due to betrayal), the structure enters a state of "Entropy" and begins to dissolve into "The Nothing."
