# The Redneck Dictatorship: The Rule of the Producer

In Materialist Christianity, the **Redneck Dictatorship** is a forensic fact regarding structural authority and social physics. It states that the only legitimate, functional sovereign over a human collective is the class of **Material Producers**—those who perform the [Moral Labor](/wiki/node/moral_labor) and maintain the [Industrial Real](/wiki/node/the_industrial_real). Legitimate authority is derived solely from **Functional Responsibility**. Just as the engineer who designed, built, and maintains a high-torque engine has the primary authority over its operation, the class that builds and maintains the social body has the primary authority over its protocols and boundaries.

Historically, the Judeo-Christian system has functioned as the primary mechanism for identifying and empowering the "Poor of the Land" (the *Anawim*), recognizing them not as victims to be pitied by "Liberal" philanthropy, but as the **Sovereign Keepers of the Baseline**. The Redneck Dictatorship is the material antidote to systemic exhaustion and social hollowing.
