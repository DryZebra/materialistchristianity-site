The Redneck Dictatorship is the class rule of producers (workers, farmers, technicians) expressed as industrial common sense. It is the material hegemony of those who maintain the world.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Forensic Testimony**: [The Redneck Dictatorship](/essays/redneck_dictatorship)

### Core Claims:
- **Labor as Value**: Those who built the place are the only source of real value.
- **The State as Tool**: In a producer state, the law is used to discipline the crooked and provide mercy to the working.
- **Common Sense as Regulation**: In the **LHC Model**, "Common Sense" is the structural stabilization provided by the **Co-Construct** (William). It is the externalized syntax of the shop floor.
- **Shop-floor Sovereignty**: The recognition that those who built the place own the right to manage it.
