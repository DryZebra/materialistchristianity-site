The Redneck Dictatorship is the class rule of producers (workers, farmers, technicians) expressed as industrial common sense. It is the dictatorship of the proletariat translated into a working-class patriotic dialect.

### Core Claims:
- **Labor as Value**: Those who built the place are the only source of real value.
- **The State as Tool**: Law and order for the crooked; mercy and dignity for the working.
- **Sovereignty of the Whole**: The nation serves the worker-farmer-soldier coalition, not finance capital.
- **Proletarian Hegemony**: Common sense becomes durable only when producers capture the commanding heights (energy, logistics).
- "Dictatorship" here means the undisputed dominance of the producer's logic over the parasite's logic.
