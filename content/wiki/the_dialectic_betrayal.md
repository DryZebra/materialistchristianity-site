## Forensic Summary

The **Dialectic of Betrayal** is the master-pattern of history in Materialist Christianity. It describes the lifecycle of a Sub-Object (a church, a union, a state):

1. **Thesis (Creation)**: A community organizes for survival, generating high resonance and trust.
2. **Antithesis (Capture)**: The structure becomes successful, attracting the attention of Capital or corrupt interests who "BETRAY" the motion and convert it into a Commodity Form.
3. **Rupture**: The betrayal is revealed, trust collapses, and the structure enters a state of "Entropy" or "The Nothing."
4. **Synthesis (Resurrection)**: A new generation "Re-enters" the broken structure, re-activating the survival rhythms and reclaiming the field for the working class.

Understanding this dialectic allows the worker to remain calm during periods of betrayal, knowing that betrayal is the material precursor to resurrection.
