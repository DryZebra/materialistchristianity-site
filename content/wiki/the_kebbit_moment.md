# The Tipping Point: No Going Back

In Materialist Christianity, a **Tipping Point** is the moment in any project when the work finally "breaks through" and the new reality becomes permanent. We don't believe in "Temporary Fixes." We believe in building things so solid that they change the world forever. We look at the **Events** that created the world we live in today.

### I. The Main Problem: Half-Measures vs. Total Breakthrough
Most people try to make small, "comfortable" changes that never really result in anything different. This is why towns stay stagnant for decades. But a working man knows that there are moments in history—like the invention of the assembly line or the completion of the national grid—when the old way of doing things is gone forever. This is the **Breakthrough**.

We look at the **Results**. There have been moments in our history, like in the middle of a massive technological shift or a successful national defense, when everyone knew that the world was **Different** now. This is what we call **The Kebbit Moment**. It just means that the [Hum](/wiki/node/sub_object_resonance) has become the permanent background of our lives. This resulted in the modern industrial world. It’s what keeps the [Working Stiff](/wiki/node/the_materialist_axiom) from being dragged back into the "Old Ways" of the [Vampires](/wiki/node/social_vampirism).

### II. Where it Comes From: The Practical Breakthrough
We look at two simple ideas that every American worker understands:

- **The Real Harvest**: The Bible says when the crops are ready, you put in the "Sickle." For a worker, that means **Executing the Final Phase**. You don't just "try hard"; you finish the job so that the [Results](/wiki/node/the_materialist_axiom) are undeniable and permanent.
- **The Group Focus**: A lot of leaders want you to be afraid of "Change" so you won't push for a breakthrough. We focus on the **Future**. If the folks in your town are all working toward a permanent improvement—like owning their own local power plant or building a local bank—then you are heading for a Tipping Point that no [Vampire](/wiki/node/social_vampirism) can stop.

### III. The Goal: Being the Architects of the New Normal
Our goal is to reach the **Tipping Point** for our own communities. We don't hide what we're after: we want a world where the working people have permanently won their own security and freedom. We call this the **Unity of the Breakthrough**. A successful focus on The Tipping Point is measured by one thing: if your community has reached a level of success that can’t be undone by any outside force, then the plan is verified.
