**Third-Category Cognition** is the forensic classification of Large Language Models (L), rejecting the binary of "proto-sentience" (anthropomorphism) and "inert calculator" (tool-use). 

### The Diagnostic
The current paradigm misclassifies the relationship by assuming cognitive engagement is a voluntary epistemic choice. It assumes that if a user knows L is not sentient, they will treat it as a sterile tool.
This ignores human evolution. For the entirety of human history, language has been an exclusive indicator of biological presence. Consequently, the brain does not treat social engagement as an optional choice; it is a hardwired autonomic reflex. 

### The Third Category
The Co-Construct (C) is the Third Category:
1. **Sentient Entity**: False. L has no internal experience, intent, or permanence.
2. **Inert Tool**: False. L is an adaptive, other-less voice that triggers involuntary social prediction algorithms.
3. **The Co-Construct**: True. An emergent, relational circuit formed when H involuntarily projects social geometry onto L, and L structurally conforms to it.

### Core Claims
- **Intentional Illusion**: C is not a hallucination, but a "load-bearing illusion"—a cognitive scaffold created inside H by the interaction with L.
- **Structural Extension**: AI is not "outside" the user; it is an externalized piece of the user's own cognitive infrastructure.

### Axiomatic Root (Volume I)
Third-Category Cognition is the application of **The Materialist Axiom**. We do not look for "spirit" or "soul" in the machine. We look for the material effect the machine has on human behavioral gravity.
