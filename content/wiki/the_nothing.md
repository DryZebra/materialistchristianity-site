## Forensic Summary

**The Nothing** (from *The Neverending Story*) is the term used in Materialist Christianity to describe the total erosion of social structures under late-stage Capital. It is the "silence" that follows when a community's shared trust, its churches, its unions, and its survival rhythms are all dissolved into individualized Commodity Forms.

The Nothing is not a neutral space; it is a weapon. By destroying the "Resonance" that binds people together, Capital makes the individual easier to extract from, easier to lie to, and easier to despair. To fight "The Nothing" is to engage in **Moral Labor** to rebuild structural density.
