The 4D Time Snake is a cognitive model that defines time not as a sequence of isolated "now-points," but as a continuous volume of cause and effect left behind by historical motion.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Forensic Testimony**: [The 4D Time Snake: Narrative Continuity](/essays/the_4d_time_snake)

### Core Claims:
- **Geometric Reality**: An object is not just where it is "now"; it is the total volume it has occupied through time. The "trail" is as materially real as the object.
- **Persistent Trail**: The parts of the "Snake" that survive the friction of social entropy are the only parts that can be called **The Real**.
- **LHC Model Integration**: In the **LHC Model**, the 4D Time Snake is the "Historical Dataset" (L) from which the "H-Operator" (Human) derives the "Co-Construct" (C).
- **Axiom 0**: Loyalties, trusts, and productive labor are the high-torque segments of the snake that bind the future to the past.
