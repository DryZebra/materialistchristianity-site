The 4D Time Snake is a cognitive model that defines time not as a sequence of isolated moments, but as a continuous "snake" of cause and effect.

### Core Claims:
- In a 4D perspective, every outcome is a convergence of parallel motions (Durability, Leverage, Certainty, Position).
- What we perceive as a single moment is a cross-section of multiple processes advancing simultaneously.
- When material conditions align, variance collapses, and the future becomes a mechanical certainty.
- "God’s Plan" is the name we give to the large-scale moral predictability of this convergence.
- The 4D snake does not care for belief; it only responds to the force applied at the correct link in the chain.
