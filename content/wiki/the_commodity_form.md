## Forensic Summary

In Materialist Christianity, a **Sub-Object** (like a holiday or a community ritual) is healthy as long as it organizes real mutual aid and survival. The **Commodity Form** is what happens when Capital captures a Sub-Object and turns it into a product for trade. When a "Holy Day" (internal logic: rest and community) becomes a "Holiday Sale" (internal logic: extraction and exchange), it has been converted into a Commodity Form.

This conversion is destructive because it strips the object of its **Behavioral Gravity**. You no longer act in alignment with your neighbors; you act in alignment with the market. The structure is still there, but it no longer carries the people.
