# The Commodity Form: The Logic of Extraction

In Materialist Christianity, the **Commodity Form** is a **Forensic Diagnosis** of a social structure that has been captured by the [Hardness of Heart](/wiki/node/the_hardness_of_heart). It is the state where a functional survival protocol—a ritual, a community bond, or a shared resource—is stripped of its relational consequence and converted into a tool for extraction. We treat this as **Structural Parasitism**, where the outer "Form" of a social law remains, but its internal "Function" (Mutual Aid, Metabolic Stability, [Shared Trust](/wiki/node/shared_trust)) is replaced by the logic of individualized exchange and profit.

We recognize the [Commodity Form](/wiki/node/commodity_as_vampire) as a primary example of a social myth created to justify the extraction of [Moral Labor](/wiki/node/moral_labor) from the human species' [Machine](/wiki/node/the_machine). It is the process by which high-density discoveries of the [Beginning](/bible/gen/1) are hollowed out to produce short-term financial gains for a parasitic interest. Materialist Christianity seeks to **Sublate** these extractive tools back into the original structural [Baseline](/wiki/node/the_bible_as_sediment), recognizing that any system built on the exploitation of the [Kingdom of Labor](/wiki/node/the_kingdom_of_labor) is forensically unstable and destined for entropic collapse into [The Nothing](/wiki/node/the_nothing).

### Dynamics of Systemic Capture
- **The House of Merchandise (John 2:16)**: Christ’s instruction—"Make not my Father's house an house of merchandise"—is the ultimate forensic audit of the Commodity Form. He identified the structural corruption of a "House of Prayer" (a site of collective coordination and [Shared Trust](/wiki/node/shared_trust)) into a site of private extraction. The cleansing of the temple was the physical removal of the parasitic "Bite" to restore the functional integrity of the social [Machine](/wiki/node/the_machine).
- **The Physics of Social Fraud**: We treat the Commodity Form as a source of **Energy-Expensive Friction**. When a community's survival tools—such as child-care, credit, or ritualized rest—are commodified, the members must expend significantly more metabolic energy to achieve the same coordinate results. This results in the progressive depletion of [Moral Capital](/wiki/node/moral_capital), as the "Trust" required for survival is continually converted into "Profit" for a non-functional interest.
- **Structural Reclamation**: We do not replace the Commodity Form with another floating "Ideal"; we return it to its original purpose of **Structural Maintenance**. If a social protocol fails to protect the material needs of the collective and instead serves primarily as an exchange-value, it is forensically identified as "Untruth." The mission is to strip away the commodity-layer to reveal the functional gears beneath, performing the work of **Resurrection**.

This is the forensic definition of **Structural Purification**: The active removal of extractive interests (Merchandise) to ensure the restoration of the social body's original metabolic utility and [Shared Resilience](/wiki/node/shared_resilience).
