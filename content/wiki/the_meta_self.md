The Meta-Self is the "observer" layer of consciousness that sees the body-self, emotional-self, and narrative-self in motion without identifying with them. It is the mezzanine of introspection.

### Core Claims:
- The Meta-Self is the higher-level process built on top of the biological "Machine."
- Integration occurs when "awareness of awareness" becomes stable.
- **You are the whole system**, including the part that sees the system.
- The shift to the Meta-Self is not a "soul" entering the body, but an increase in introspective resolution.
- It is the prerequisite for "Self-Modification": you cannot change what you cannot observe.
