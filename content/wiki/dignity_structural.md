## Forensic Summary

**Structural Dignity** is the residue of labor that results in the preservation of the community. It is not an internal feeling or a "right," but a physical weight—**Kavod**—carried by those whose lives act as the pillars of history.

## Morphological Grounding: Kavod

The word usually translated as "Glory" (**Kavod**) in scripture literally means **Weight** or **Heaviness**. Dignity is the physical mass of a soul that has become reliable through years of survival-labor. It is the weight of a person who "stays in the harness" while others flee into entropy.

## Forensic Expansion

As explored in the [[01_the_electrician_creed]], Christianity is the structure that preserved the category of "Value" for ordinary folks who had nothing but their labor. It is the grid that gave mass to the individual when empire and economy threatened to dissolve them. It is the realization that "Christianity works" as a mechanism for documenting individual dignity through group survival.
