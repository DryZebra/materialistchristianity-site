## Forensic Summary

A **Ritual of Continuity** is the hollow inverse of a **Ritual of Consequence**. These are actions that people perform to feel like a community exists ("The mask of life") while they are actually sitting inside **The Nothing**. Examples include corporatized holidays, "performative activism" that results in no policy change, or a church service that contains no mutual aid.

These rituals are dangerous because they provide a false sense of **Resonance**. They allow the "Vampire" (Capital) to consume the community's time and energy without ever producing the **Moral Capital** or **Shared Trust** necessary for real survival.
