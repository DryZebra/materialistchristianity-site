## Forensic Summary

**Corrupted Memory** is the result of structural **Entropy**. It occurs when a community remembers *what* it does (rituals) but forgets *why* it does it (survival necessity). Memory is corrupted when it shifts from being a data set for **Historical Alignment** into being a sentimental "vibe" or aesthetic preference.

In Materialist Christianity, a corrupted memory is what allows a Sub-Object to be captured by the **Commodity Form**. If people only remember the "feeling" of a church and not its material function as a mutual aid network, it is easily sold back to them as a product rather than a survival structure.
