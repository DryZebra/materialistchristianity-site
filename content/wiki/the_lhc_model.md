# The Power Plant: The Momentum of the Folks

In Materialist Christianity, the **Power Plant** model is how we think about the economy of a strong town. It’s not about "Investors" in New York getting a cut; it’s about a **Circular Momentum** where the hard work of the [Reliable Men](/wiki/node/the_church_as_structural_anchor) flows into the local shops, the local schools, and the local homes. Whatever is produced by the community *stays* in the community.

### I. The Main Problem: The Leak vs. The Continuous Flow
Most people live in towns where the money and the energy "Leak Out." You work for a big chain store, and the profit goes to a billionaire halfway across the world. This is why our towns are hollowing out. But a working man knows that in a real "Power Plant" (the LHC model), the energy stays in the loop. The momentum built today makes tomorrow's work even easier.

We look at the **Results**. There have been moments in our history, like in a tight-knit farming community with its own local silos and local banks, when the town was a **Self-Sustaining Loop**. The work produced the local wealth, and the local wealth produced the next generation of workers. This resulted in the most independent and stable families in history. It’s what keeps the [Working Stiff](/wiki/node/the_materialist_axiom) from being drained by the [Vampires](/wiki/node/social_vampirism).

### II. The Scriptural Anchor
- **Psalm 16:6**: "The lines are fallen unto me in pleasant places; yea, I have a goodly heritage."
- **Unique Mapping**: Christianity uniquely identifies "Heritage" not as a memory or a biological line, but as the **Surveyed Boundaries** of the operation. In industrial terms, the "Lines" are the **Operational Territory** (the LHC) where the worker has the final say. A "Goodly Heritage" is a **High-Performance Shop** that has been correctly bounded against external parasites. The "Lines" define where the [Vampire's](/wiki/node/social_vampirism) authority ends and the worker's sovereignty begins.

### III. The Goal: Building a Town that Rules Itself
Our goal is to turn every small town into a **Production Loop**. We don't hide what we're after: we want a world where the working people own the energy and the wealth they produce. We call this the **Unity of the Momentum**. A successful focus on The Power Plant is measured by one thing: if your community is getting richer and more stable every day without any help from outside bankers, then the plan is verified. The [Church](/wiki/node/the_church_as_structural_anchor) is the "Control Room" for the community energy.
