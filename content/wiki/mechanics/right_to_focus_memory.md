# The Right to Focus and Memory

In Materialist Christianity, the **Right to Focus** is the technical defense of the human mind against the globalist algorithm. It identifies that your **Attention (The Forehead)** is a physical resource that the extractor is trying to hollow out.

## Forensic Analysis: The Extraction of Attention
The globalist algorithm wants your constant "Attention" to serve its price-signaling.
1.  **The Hollowing of Focus**: By keeping you distracted with digital "Ghosts" (notifications, news, and infinite scrolling), the extractor prevents you from doing **Real Work**. This is a materialist theft of your time. 
2.  **The Deletion of Memory**: A community that cannot remember its own history of survival is easier to extract. The algorithm harrows your memory to keep you in a state of "Now."
3.  **The Reclaiming of the Forehead**: To reclaim your focus is to physically put down the phone and pick up the tool. It is the technical act of directing your attention to the **Neighbor and the Job Site**.

## Summary
The **Focus Diagnostic** identifies that our minds belong to us. To be a Materialist Christian is to protect your **Right to Focus** and ensure your memory serves the Kingdom, not the extractor.
