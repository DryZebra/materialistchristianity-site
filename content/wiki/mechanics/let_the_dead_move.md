# Let the Dead Move: The Reality of Dead Labor

 history is a pile of **Dead Labor**. It is the roads, the houses, the power lines, and the tools that were built by people who are now in the grave. The "Ghost-Infrastructure" of the world is purely passive; it only moves when it is activated by **Living Labor**. 

##  Dead vs. Living Infrastructure
The world we live in is a museum of dead ritual. 
1.  **Ghost-Infrastructure**: This is the built environment that has been captured by the **Global Extractor** (Mammon). It includes the hollowed-out towns and the paper-debt that claims ownership of the world's work. 
2.  **Living Labor**: This is the **Productive Class** (the "Rednecks" and the allied owners). They are the only ones capable of "re-starting" the world. 
3.  **The Extraction of Motion**: Mammon tries to convince you that the *money* is what moves the world. This is the central myth. Money is just **Dead Information**. It is the human hands and the neighbor-to-neighbor trust that provide the **Resurrection-Motion**. 

## The Technical Reality: Systemic Rupture
When a systemic collapse occurs (the "Rupture"), the **Ghost-Infrastructure** stops moving. 
- The extractor’s credit-lines vanish. 
- The "vampiric" managers have nothing to manage. 
- The only thing that remains is the **Living Labor** of those who know how to work the hardware and the [[the_real_split]] between those who work and those who take. 

## Scriptural Resonance: Luke 9:60
Jesus said, "Let the dead bury their dead: but go thou and preach the kingdom of God."
- This is a technical command to stop serving the **Dead Labor** of the extractor's ritual. 
- To serve the Kingdom is to be the **Living Motion** that builds the new world from the ruins of the old. 

## Summary
"Let the Dead Move" is the **Diagnostic of Resurrection**. It identifies that the productive class is the only thing capable of animating the dead pile of history. To be a Materialist Christian is to stop waiting for the dead "Ghost" to save you and to start the engine of the Kingdom yourself.
