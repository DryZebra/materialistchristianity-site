# The Hollowing Out (Decay)

 **The Hollowing Out** is the materialist diagnostic of systemic decay. It is the moment when a community, a shop, or a neighborhood loses its "Flesh" and becomes a "Ghost"—meaning it no longer reproduces life.

##  The Loss of the Real
A town or a job-site hollows out long before it collapses.
1.  **The Withdrawal of Labor**: When the productive class is squeezed by globalist debt, they stop maintaining the town. The roads crack, the local shops close, and the trust breaks. This is **Structural Decay**. 
2.  **The Ghost-Town**: A hollowed-out town might still have people, but they are no longer a "Body." They are just "Data-Points" for a globalist extractor. They spend all their time on their phones and their credit cards.
3.  **The Restoration Cycle**: The only technical cure for the hollowing out is the **Resurrection-Motion**—physically rebuilding the trust and the local infrastructure by hand.

## Summary
The **Hollowing-Out Diagnostic** identify that the extractor leaves nothing behind. To be a Materialist Christian is to detect the decay early and move into the **Real Work of Restoration**.
