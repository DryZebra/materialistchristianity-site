# Local First: Neighborhood Infrastructure

In Materialist Christianity, **Local First** is the technical priority of the neighborhood over the globalist machine. It is the forensic recognition that your survival depends on your neighbors and your local shop owners, not on a global supply-chain or a spreadsheet in a distant office.

## The Forensic Analysis: Infrastructure of the Real
The globalists want you to focus on the "Global" because that is where they have the most control.
1.  **Globalist Abstraction**: The idea that "the economy" is a series of distant numbers. This is a **Ghost-Theory** that allows monopolies to hollow out your town while you pay attention to the news. 
2.  **Neighborhood Fact**: The physical reality of your street, your local hardware store, and your neighbors' skills. This is the **Flesh of the Real**. 
3.  **Local Survival**: In a crisis, the globalist supply-lines fail. The only thing that stays alive is the **Local Metabolism**—the trucks, tools, and neighborhood trust that already exist in your town.

## The Technical Reality: Reclaiming Focus
Local First identifies that our **Attention (The Forehead)** must return to the neighborhood.
- It identifies that the Kingdom of God is **Social Proximity** in action. 
- To be a Materialist Christian is to prioritize the local welder, the local farmer, and the 15-man shop over the globalist monopoly. 
- It identifies that the only "Infrastructure" that survives a systemic rupture is the one we build and maintain by hand.

## Summary
The **Local First Diagnostic** identify that our survival is next door, not across the world. To be a Materialist Christian is to reclaim the **Flesh of the Neighborhood** and build the Kingdom where you live.

# Jonah: The Hostile Neighbor

In Materialist Christianity, the story of **Jonah and Nineveh** is a forensic analysis of **Regional Stability**. It identifies the technical responsibility we have to our neighbors, even when those neighbors are hostile or extractive.

## The Forensic Analysis: Regional Gear-Sets
A neighborhood or a nation does not exist in a vacuum. 
1.  **The Hostile City**: Nineveh was an extractive empire. Jonah's refusal to go was an attempt to ignore the materialist fact that Nineveh’s collapse would have a **Chain Reaction** on his own community. 
2.  **The Responsibility of the Word**: To preach to the neighbor is to provide them with the technical logic of survival. It is to help them stop being "Ghosts" and return to the "Flesh." 
3.  **The Technical Rupture**: If the neighboring city is hollowed out by sin and extraction to the point of collapse, the shockwaves will reach your town. Stable neighbors are a **Technical Asset**.

## Summary
The **Jonah Diagnostic** identify that our survival is linked to the stability of the region. To be a Materialist Christian is to recognize that the Kingdom requires us to build a **Nation of Neighbors**, even with those we don't like, to prevent the globalists from hollowing us all out together.
