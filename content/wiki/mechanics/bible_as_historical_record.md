# Bible as Historical Record

The Bible is a forensic archive of historical field reports documenting the development and survival of human social technology.

## The Technical Reality: How it Works
Materialist Christianity rejects the reduction of Scripture to "Myth" or "Fable." We treat the Bible as a record of **Material Facts**. The "Supernatural" is often a placeholder for a mechanism that was not yet technically measured by the observers, but the **Physical Consequences** are what matter to the forensic auditor.

In a 15-man fabrication shop, you don't use a "Story" to repair a machine; you use a service manual. The Bible is the service manual for **Human Synchronization**. It documents exactly what happens when tribes over-extract their neighbors (Sodom) and what happens when they achieve total metabolic coordination (Acts). We value the record for its **Structural Consistency** across thousands of years of human survival.

## Scriptural Grounding
**Luke 1:1-4**. Luke explicitly states his intent is to provide an "orderly account" based on "eyewitnesses." He is performing a **Forensic Audit** of recent social history. He is not writing a myth; he is filing a technical report for a "Most Excellent" recipient.
