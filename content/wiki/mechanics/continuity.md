# Continuity

In the Materialist Christianity framework, **Continuity** refers specifically to **[Cognitive Continuity Rights](/wiki/mechanics/cognitive_continuity_rights)**. It is the mechanical requirement that a social subject maintains a persistent, un-corrupted memory of their own labor and consequence across time.

---

# The Haunted Object

**The Haunted Object** refers to a material entity that has been assigned an erratic, "animist" value rather than a stable, social protocol value. It is a failure of standardization. (See: **[Animism To Protocol](/wiki/testimonies/animism_to_protocol)**)

---

# Tectonic Luck

**Tectonic Luck** is a diagnostic term for the "Small Games" or random variables that occur on the local scale before they are metabolized by the **[Social Reinvestment Loop](/wiki/mechanics/social_reinvestment_loop)**. 

---

# The Substrate Body

**The Substrate Body** refers to the biological and environmental "Hardware" upon which the human social Operating System is installed. (See: **[The Human Structure Interface](/wiki/mechanics/the_human_structure_interface)**)
