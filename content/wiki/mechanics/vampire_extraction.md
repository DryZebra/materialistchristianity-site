# The Vampire (Extraction)

In Materialist Christianity, **The Vampire** is the forensic archetype of the extractive layer. It identifies any system that pulls "Flesh" (life and labor) from the neighborhood without returning anything real.

## Forensic Analysis: Sucking the Town Dry
Globalist finance is the primary vampire.
1.  **Interests and Fees**: Every dollar of interest paid to a global bank is a drop of "Blood" pulled from your town. It is **Non-Productive Extraction**. 
2.  **The Hollowing Out**: A community under the vampire's command becomes "Hollow." The people work more, but they have less. They are tired, distracted, and dependent. 
3.  **The Master of the Debt**: The globalist extractor uses your debt as a "Ghost-Chain" to command your **Work (The Hand)**. 

## Summary
The **Vampire Diagnostic** identify the predator. To be a Materialist Christian is to reclaim your labor and ensure that every drop of your sweat stays in the **Kingdom of Labor**.
