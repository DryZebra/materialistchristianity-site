# The Materialist Christian Creed

This is a clear, professional summary of the materialist reality of the faith. It identifies what we recognize as true about our labor, our community, and the globalists.

## 🏛️ The Three Realities
1.  **Labor is the Engine**: We recognize that only **Human Labor** moves the world. Money is just data; credit cards are just debt. The Kingdom is built by hands.
2.  **Community is the Infrastructure**: We recognize that our **Neighbors** and the **Allied Owners** in our town are our only critical survival assets. We synchronize our trucks, our tools, and our trust to survive.
3.  **Monopolies are the Extractor**: We recognize that globalist monopolies are hollow systems that live off our work. We identify them as the primary hurdle to our local survival.

## 🚩 The Two Commands
1.  **Partake in the Flesh**: We choose the **Real Work** over the hollowed-out economy of the extractors. 
2.  **Protect the Container**: We maintain the borders of our responsibility, ensuring that our labor stays within our community to help each other stay alive.

## Summary
The **Creed** is the technical alignment of our focus. To be a Materialist Christian is to live in the **Flesh of the Real**, build the **Kingdom of Labor**, and refuse the **Ghost-Theories** of the extractors.
