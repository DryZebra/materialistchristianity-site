# Systemic Collapse (Rupture)

In Materialist Christianity, **Systemic Collapse** is the forensic recognition that extractive systems (globalist monopolies, financial debt-cycles) eventually hollow out to the point of breaking. It identifies that the Kingdom must be physically ready to survive the day the "Ghost-Supply Chain" fails.

## Forensic Analysis: The Moment of Failure
A collapse is a materialist fact, not a "doomsday prophecy."
1.  **The Hollowing Out**: When a system is entirely based on **Credit and Debt** (Mammon), it stops being a "Flesh" structure. At a certain point, the extraction of labor is so total that the system cannot even maintain itself. 
2.  **The Rupture**: The moment the supply-lines stop and the credit cards fail. This is the **Structural Rupture**.
3.  **The Floor of the Real**: After the collapse, the only things left are the **Neighborhood Tools**, the **Local Shops**, and **Neighborhood Trust**. 

## Summary
The **Collapse Diagnostic** identify that extraction is finite. To be a Materialist Christian is to build the **Local Alternative** that stays alive long after the globalist machine has broken.

# Moving After a Collapse

## Forensic Analysis: Re-starting the Local Gear

1.  **The Rejection of Despair**: Despair is a "Ghost-Emotion" that prevents the productive class from working. The materialist diagnostic is **Motion**.
2.  **The Re-activation of synchronization**: Rebuilding starts by finding your neighbor and sharing what is left. It is the technical act of **Synchronization**. 
3.  **The Role of the Expert**: In a collapse, the **Worker** (the plumber, the electrician, the neighbor with the tractor) becomes the **Leader**. 

## Summary
The **Re-entry Diagnostic** identify the work of survival. To be a Materialist Christian is to be the **First One Back to Work** when the world restarts.

# Temporary Infrastructure (Scaffolding)

In Materialist Christianity, **Temporary Infrastructure** is the technical acts of building "Support Structures" for the community while the larger system is failing. It identifies that the Kingdom is often built in stages.

## Forensic Analysis: Building through the Rupture
We don't build the whole nation at once; we build the **Scaffolding** first.
1.  **Support Nodes**: This includes things like local tool libraries, neighborhood kitchens, and shared generators. They aren't permanent, but they allow the **Real Work** to continue. 
2.  **The Local Gear-Set**: Scaffolding identifies that the **Small Scale** is the foundation of the **Large Scale**. 
3.  **Resilience under Pressure**: Infrastructure that is built by hand is harder to extract because the globalist doesn't even see it as "Real."

## Summary
The **Scaffolding Diagnostic** identify our steps. To be a Materialist Christian is to build what is needed **Now** to protect the neighborhood's survival tomorrow.

# Neighborly Distance (Social Proximity)

In Materialist Christianity, **Neighborly Distance** is the materialist recognition that your physical proximity to your neighbors is your most critical survival asset. It is the forensic rejection of the globalist "Digital Ghost."

## Forensic Analysis: Physical Trust
You cannot synchronize with someone you can only see on a screen. 
1.  **The Range of the Hand**: Real work happens within **Walking Distance** (the physical range of the neighbor). 
2.  **The Breakdown of Abstraction**: Globalist managers want us to "Connect" across the world while ignoring the family next door. This is the **Hollowing of Social Life**. 
3.  **Local Synchronization**: Trust is built at the fence, in the breakroom, and at the shop. This physical proximity is the **Structural Bond** of the productive class.

## Summary
The **Proximity Diagnostic** identify that the Kingdom is **Local**. To be a Materialist Christian is to reclaim your neighbors' name and pick up the tools in your own street.
