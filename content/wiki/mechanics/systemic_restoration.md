# Systemic Restoration (Resurrection)

In Materialist Christianity, the "Resurrection" is the technical process of **Restarting the Real World** after it has been hollowed out by extraction. It is the forensic analysis of life returning to the community through labor.

## Forensic Analysis: Rebuilding the Flesh
When a system collapses (Lazarus, or a town in a crisis), the "Ghost" of the extractor has already left. 
1.  **The Call to Rise**: Restoration is the command for the productive class to pick up their tools and start moving again. 
2.  **Structural Integrity**: Rebuilding is not an "Idea"; it is the physical act of repairing the roads, the homes, and the trust. 
3.  **The Victory over the Nothing**: Resurrection is the technical win over **Decay**. It identifies that the productive class is the only thing capable of surviving a systemic rupture.

## Summary
The **Restoration Diagnostic** identify the future. To be a Materialist Christian is to be the **Restoration Crew** for the world, rebuilding the Kingdom on the ruins of the globalist machine.
