# Forensic Audit Method

The **Forensic Audit Method** is the operative diagnostic tool of Materialist Christianity. It rejects the standard theological practice of "Spiritualization"—the process of abstracting material events into moral or allegorical lessons—and replaces it with **Mechanical Reconstruction**.

### The Failure of Traditional Translation
Standard translations (and generative AI defaults) treat the Biblical text as a literary or religious narrative. They look for *intent*, *sentiment*, and *morality*. This results in "floaty" abstractions that provide no traction in the Industrial Real.

### The Superior Mechanical Diagnostic
A Forensic Audit treats the text as a **Hardware Log** or a **Circuit Diagram** of human social organization. 

1.  **Identify the Friction**: What was the material problem being solved? (e.g., famine, territorial conflict, inheritance breakdown).
2.  **Locate the Infrastructure**: What material systems were established? (e.g., wells, altars/data-nodes, genealogies/version-control).
3.  **Trace the Torque**: How did these actions increase the agency and survival-torque of the early Logos-bearing nodes?

### Case Study: Jacob's Well
- **Idealist Interpretation**: A place of meeting and spiritual refreshment.
- **Forensic Audit**: A critical piece of hydraulic infrastructure in a contested extraction zone. The "blessing" is the successful defense and maintenance of this infrastructure against interfacial friction (Philistines).

### Forward Mapping
All future expansions of the Wiki and the Archive must pass through this audit. If a node cannot be grounded in material social mechanics, it is a "ghost in the machine" and must be pruned or rewritten.
