# Materialist Christianity vs. Christian Scientific

Various "Christian Scientific" movements (such as Christian Science or New Thought) attempt to bring the rigor of science to the realm of faith. While Materialist Christianity (MC) values technical rigor, it identifies a categorical error in the "Scientific" religious approach.

### The Forensic Divergence
*   **Christian Scientific is Metaphysical**: It often treats "Mind" or "Spirit" as a set of hidden laws that can be manipulated to produce physical effects (healing, wealth, peace). It is essentially "Magic" with scientific branding. It attempts to use "Spirit" to bypass the hardware.
*   **Materialist Christianity is Axiomatic**: It does not try to use Spirit to bypass hardware; it uses **Software Analysis** (Scripture) to **Diagnostic the Hardware** (The social/physical reality). It uses the **Atheist Axiom** to ensure that no "Ghost" is blamed for what is actually a mechanical failure in the group's synchronization.

### Common Ground
Both reject blind institutional dogma. Both believe that faith should have measurable, objective results in the world.

### The Mechanical Necessity of MC
Christian Scientific frameworks often fail the **15-man Shop Test** because they rely on individual "Mind-sets." If an individual's "Mind" fails, the community has no **Metabolic Floor** to catch them.

Materialist Christianity is necessary because it reveals that the **Result** is not produced by "Mind," but by **Coordinated Motion**. It provides the **Apostolic Hardware Diagnostics** that allow a group to identify *why* a distribution failed or *why* trust was broken, without resorting to spiritual excuses. MC turns "Scientific" into a **Forensic Requirement**, not a metaphysical claim.
