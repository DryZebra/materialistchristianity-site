# Abiogenesis: The Flesh Origin

In Materialist Christianity, **Abiogenesis** (the origin of life from non-life) is the mechanical proof that the "Word" appeared in the physical world without magical idealism. It identifies that the faith is built on the **Materialist Floor** of reality.

## The Forensic Analysis: Life as Work
Life is not a "Ghost-Spirit" added to matter; it is the **Work of Matter**. 
1.  **The Word and the Flesh**: Materialist Christianity recognizes that the "Word" (the Information/Logic of Life) and the "Flesh" (The physical matter) are a **Single Gear**. 
2.  **The Master of the Real**: Christ is the master of this materialist process. He identifies the physical world as the only place where truth exists. 
3.  **The Refusal of Magical Thinking**: We do not need a "Ghost-Theory" to explain life. The physicality of life is proof enough. It identifies that our faith is grounded in the **Historical Fact** of the world.

## Summary
The **Abiogenesis Diagnostic** identify that we are a people of the **Flesh**. To be a Materialist Christian is to stop looking for magical "Ghosts" and start recognizing the technical glory of the material world we build and maintain.
