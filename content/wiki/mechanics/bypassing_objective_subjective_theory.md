# Bypassing Objective Subjective Theory

Truth is the physical result of an action, regardless of the internal feelings of the observer or the abstract theories of the designer.

## The Technical Reality: How it Works
In a 15-man fabrication shop, "Theory" doesn't weld the pipe. The weld is either structurally sound or it isn't. The "Subjective" (the welder's mood) and the "Objective" (the blueprint) are both subordinate to the **Physical Test**. 

Materialist Christianity identifies that most human debate is a "Ghost-War" between subjective feelings and objective abstractions. We bypass this by focusing on **Structural Integrity**. If a community protocol (like the Sabbath or the Tithe) keeps a family from collapsing under market pressure, it is **True**. It doesn't matter if you "believe" in it or if you have a "theory" for why it works. The reality is found in the fact that the family stayed upright.

## Scriptural Grounding
**Matthew 7:16-20** ("By their fruits ye shall know them"). Christ does not ask for a subjective confession or an objective doctrine; He points to the physical output (the fruit) as the sole validator of the system's reality. The "Fruit" is the material evidence of the structure's health.
