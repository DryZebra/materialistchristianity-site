# Bypassing the Objective/Subjective Binary

In many academic circles, reality is split into "objective" (external facts) and "subjective" (internal feelings). Materialist Christianity bypasses this binary, recognizing it as a **Ghost-Theory** that obscures the technical mechanics of survival. 

## The Forensic Split: Tool vs. Theory
For the productive class (the "Redneck" population and the allied shop owners), the binary is not between objective and subjective. The binary is between **What Works** and **What Hollows Out**.
1.  **The Objective Myth**: The idea that reality is a set of "disinterested facts" that can be managed from a distance. This is the myth of the **Global Extractor** (Mammon), who wants to manage your life via a spreadsheet. 
2.  **The Subjective Myth**: The idea that faith or value is "just how you feel." This is the myth of the **Liberal Idiosyncrasy**, allowing the extractor to keep the material world while you keep your internal "feelings."
3.  **The Materialist Third Way**: The recognition that a tool, a road, or a community-trust is neither purely "objective" nor "subjective"—it is a **Survival Asset**. 

## The Technical Reality: The Job Site
On a job site, you don't ask if a hammer is "objectively real" or "subjectively useful." You recognize its mechanical function. 
- If the tool breaks, the job stops. 
- If the crew doesn't trust each other, the job stops. 
- These are not "feelings"; they are the **Mechanics of Motion**. 

## Scriptural Resonance: The Fruit of the Spirit
Jesus did not teach his disciples "Objective and Subjective Theory." He taught them to look at the **Fruit** (Matthew 7:16).
- "By their fruits ye shall know them."
- The "Fruit" is the materialist diagnostic. It is the visible evidence of the work. If the fruit is rot, the structure is failing. 

## Summary
Bypassing the binary is the act of **Refocusing on the Real**. It identifies that the extractor uses "Objective Management" to pull value away and "Subjective Faith" to keep you from demanding it back. To be a Materialist Christian is to demand the **Flesh of the Real**.
