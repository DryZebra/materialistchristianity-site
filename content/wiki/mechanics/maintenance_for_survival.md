# Maintenance for Survival

In Materialist Christianity, **Maintenance** is not a chore; it is the technical defense of the community against **Decay (Entropy)**. It is the forensic analysis of keeping the "Flesh" alive in a hollowed-out world.

## Forensic Analysis: Protecting the Local Assets
The globalist extractor wants us to "Consume and Discard." 
1.  **Maintenance as Resistance**: By fixing what we already have (trucks, homes, neighborly trust), we withdraw from the globalist’s market. We refuse to be extracted for a "Replacement Product." 
2.  **Preserving the Integrity**: Maintenance is the technical act of ensuring the reproduction of our community. If the local shop or the neighborhood gear fails, the globalists win. 
3.  **The Skill of Restoration**: To maintain is to be like Christ—the **Restorer of the World**.

## Summary
The **Maintenance Diagnostic** identify our defense. To be a Materialist Christian is to take responsibility for the **Structural Integrity** of your town and your neighbors.
