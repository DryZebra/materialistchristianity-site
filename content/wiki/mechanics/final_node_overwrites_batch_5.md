# Credit as Happiness (Borrowed Happiness)

In Materialist Christianity, **Credit as Happiness** is the forensic recognition that borrowing money to consume is not "Growth"—it is the **Extraction of Future Energy**. It is the materialist truth that the globalist extractor wants you to feel happy today using tomorrow's work.

## Forensic Analysis: The Theft of the Future
Borrowed happiness is a "Ghost-Emotion."
1.  **The Extraction of Tomorrow**: When you use a **Credit Card** to buy junk you don't need, the extractor is already hollowing out your future labor. You are happy for a day, but you are a slave for a month. 
2.  **The Loop of Debt**: This is the primary tool of the **Rival Master (Mammon)**. If you are always running to catch up with your debt, you stop having the energy to build the **Kingdom**.
3.  **The Mechanical Floor**: Real happiness is the **Result of Work**. It is the solid weld, the fixed roof, and the full table. It is the **Flesh of the Real**.

## Summary
The **Credit Diagnostic** identify the lure. To be a Materialist Christian is to refuse the "Ghost-Happiness" of debt and find your joy in the **Productive Motion** of the Kingdom.
