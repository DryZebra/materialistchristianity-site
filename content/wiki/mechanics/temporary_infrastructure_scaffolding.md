# Temporary Infrastructure (Scaffolding)

In Materialist Christianity, **Temporary Infrastructure** is the technical acts of building "Support Structures" for the community while the larger system is failing. It identifies that the Kingdom is often built in stages.

## Forensic Analysis: Building through the Rupture
We don't build the whole nation at once; we build the **Scaffolding** first.
1.  **Support Nodes**: This includes things like local tool libraries, neighborhood kitchens, and shared generators. They aren't permanent, but they allow the **Real Work** to continue. 
2.  **The Local Gear-Set**: Scaffolding identifies that the **Small Scale** is the foundation of the **Large Scale**. 
3.  **Resilience under Pressure**: Infrastructure that is built by hand is harder to extract because the globalist doesn't even see it as "Real."

## Summary
The **Scaffolding Diagnostic** identify our steps. To be a Materialist Christian is to build what is needed **Now** to protect the neighborhood's survival tomorrow.
