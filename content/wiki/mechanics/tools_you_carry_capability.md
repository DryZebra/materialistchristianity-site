# The Tools You Carry

 **The Tools You Carry** is the forensic recognition that your physical capability is your primary technical identity. It identifies that the Kingdom is built by those who are **Ready to Move**.

##  Physical Capability
Wealth is not what you have in the bank; it is what you can **Do with Your Hands**.
1.  **The Ready Worker**: A man with a truck and a welder is a **Mobile Asset** for the Kingdom. He can synchronize with his neighbors immediately. 
2.  **The Defense of Skill**: Your skill is a resource that the globalist cannot easily extract unless he "Buys" your time. Protect your skill as your **Primary Infrastructure**. 
3.  **The Master of the Real**: Christ chose disciples who were **Workers** (Fishers, laborers). He identified that those who know how to work are the only ones capable of building the Kingdom.

## Summary
The **Tools Diagnostic** identify our strength. To be a Materialist Christian is to sharpen your tools, refine your skills, and be ready to move within the **Flesh of the Real**.
