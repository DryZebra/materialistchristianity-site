# Patterns of Survival (Book of Fractals)

 **Patterns of Survival** identify that the same materialist laws of the individual worker apply to the neighborhood, the nation, and the world. It is the forensic logic of **Structural Continuity**.

##  The Scale of the Real
What works for a 15-man shop works for the Kingdom.
1.  **Synchronization as Law**: A shop won't survive if the workers are hollowing each other out. A nation won't survive if the globalists are hollowing out the town. The **Pattern is the Same**. 
2.  **The Infrastructure of Truth**: Faith, trust, and productive work are the technical requirements at every scale. 
3.  **The Recognition of the Pattern**: To see the Kingdom is to see that the **Flesh of the Neighbor** is more real than the globalist spreadsheet.

## Summary
The **Pattern Diagnostic** identify the roadmap. To be a Materialist Christian is to apply the same **Honesty with Your Tools** to every level of your life.
