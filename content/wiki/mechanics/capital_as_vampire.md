# Capital as Vampire: The Diagnostic of Extraction

In Materialist Christianity, calling capital a "vampire" is not a moral insult or a poetic metaphor; it is a **forensic diagnostic** of how the system of global extraction (**Mammon**) actually functions. It describes a structure that has no independent "Life" and can only achieve motion by hollowing out the real, productive work of the nation.

## The Forensic Analysis: Living vs. Dead Labor
The "Vampiric" nature of extraction is the technical reason for the hollowing out of communities.
1.  **Living Labor**: This is the "Working Class" in the materialist sense—the people whose physical and intellectual effort actually reproduces social life. They provide the living motion that builds, repairs, and maintains the world.
2.  **Dead Labor (The Vampire)**: Karl Marx identified capital as "Dead Labor" that, vampire-like, only lives by sucking living labor. In the **Professional Materialist** lens, this is accurate. **Mammon** is a pile of "Ghost-Labor" (accumulated profit, credit, debt) that commands the living to move for its benefit.
3.  **Mechanical Life-Theft**: Extraction is the process of taking the "Motion" from a productive shop or town and converting it into abstract numbers. This process leaves the town physically "hollowed-out" while the extractor’s spreadsheets appear to "grow."

## The Technical Reality: Mammon as Rival Master (Matthew 6:24)
The diagnostic of the vampire reveals the **Rival Master**. Mammon is a structural god that demands absolute loyalty to the spreadsheet over loyalty to the neighbors or the nation. 
- **The Extraction of Trust**: When the extractor demands profit from a shop while providing zero tools or safety, he is hollowing out the **Trust-Capital** of that community.
- **The Accusation of Obsolecence**: The extractor must label the productive class as "backwards" or "reactionary." This is a structural defense—it obscures the fact that the productive class is the only thing capable of direct survival, while the extractor is purely parasitic.

## Biblical Diagnosis: James 5:4
"Behold, the hire of the labourers... which is of you kept back by fraud, crieth."

This is the physical "cry" of the hollowing process itself. It is the materialist detection of a theft of life-rhythm.

## Summary
The **Diagnostic of the Vampire** identifies the **Global Extractor (Mammon)** as the primary friction against human survival. To serve the Kingdom is to separate from this extractive cycle and return the life-motion to those who actually do the work.
