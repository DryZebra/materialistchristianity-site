## Forensic Summary

**Moral Communism** is the ultimate goal of the Materialist Christianity project. It is defined not as a political theory, but as a **Structural High-Density State**. It occurs when a community has built enough **Moral Capital** and **Shared Trust** that every Sub-Object (resource, ritual, law) is dedicated to the survival of every member.

In this state, the "Behavioral Gravity" of the community is so strong that no individual can be extracted by Capital. It is the material realization of the early church's communal living—not as a one-time gesture, but as a permanent, high-torque industrial survival rhythm.
