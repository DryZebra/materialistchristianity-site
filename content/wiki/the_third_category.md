## Forensic Summary

In standard philosophy, things are often split into **Subjects** (minds/feelings/will) and **Objects** (physical matter). Materialist Christianity identifies **The Third Category**: the domain of **Sub-Objects**.

Sub-objects are neither purely mental nor purely material. A law, a currency, or a ritual is not just an idea (Subject), nor is it just the ink on a page (Object). It is a relational structure that exists only when collective behavior (Resonance) is held in tension across time. It is "The Third Category" because it has the durability of an object (it persists without your individual belief) but the responsiveness of a subject (it can be changed or corrupted through collective motion).
