1.  **The Header (Reshit)**: This is not a "moment in time" but the establishing of the **Temporal Vector**. It is the initialization of the sequence.
2.  **The Shaping (Bara)**: The act of "creation" is described as *shaping* or *carving*—the reduction of high-variance potential into defined geometry.
3.  **The Laws (Elohim)**: We diagnose "Elohim" not as a bearded man in the sky, but as the **Total Sum of Necessary Forces** (The Pillars of the Real). These are the subject-independent laws that govern the motion of matter.
4.  **The Dichotomy**: The initialization establishes the two planes of existence:
    - **The Expansion (Shamayim)**: The space of potential and atmosphere.
    - **The Substrate (Aretz)**: The ground of labor and resistance.
